Final Fantasy 5

Final Fantasy 5 is an entry in the extremely popular Final Fantasy series of video games. However, 5 was not released in the United States until seven years after it was published in Japan, due to fears that it would sell badly. The gameplay mechanics and plot elements of this title would prove some of the most enduring in the series.

The World of Final Fantasy 5

  • The game is set in a world with strong medieval fantasy elements.
  • However, the heroes eventually discover an ancient civilization that possessed advanced technology.
  • Many countries in the game setting also possess some knowledge of machinery.
  • The world of Final Fantasy 5 is sustained by the power of four elemental crystals (representing the classical elements of fire, water, wind, and earth).

The Cast of Final Fantasy 5,

  1. Bartz, a nomadic adventurer.
  2. Lenna, the princess of the Kingdom of Tycoon
  3. Galuf, an elderly gentleman searching for his identity
  4. Faris, a pirate captain
  5. Exdeath, a powerful dark spirit who plots to destroy the world.
  6. Krile, the princess of the Kingdom of Bal
